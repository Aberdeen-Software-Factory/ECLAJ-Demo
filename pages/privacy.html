Privacy and cookies

This prototype is run by the UK-CLAD project team at the University of Aberdeen. It explains how the site may use analytics cookies and what information those analytics would collect if enabled.

Analytics cookies

The site may use Google Analytics if you accept analytics cookies. Analytics will stay off unless the cookie consent banner records that you have accepted analytics cookies.

If analytics are enabled, we intend to track page views, dataset and download clicks, estimator spreadsheet downloads, coding book clicks, and survey clicks. These events help the project team understand which prototype features are being used.

Google Analytics and privacy

Google Analytics is provided by Google. Google may process analytics information according to its own terms and privacy notices. Read the Google Privacy Policy for more information.
