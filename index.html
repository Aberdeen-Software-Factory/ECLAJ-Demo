UK Competition Litigation and Collective Actions Dataset

The study provides a longitudinal analysis of damages actions in the UK, focusing in particular on collective actions, access to justice, and litigation trends over time. It draws on an original dataset and aims to offer both descriptive insights and a basis for comparative work in the future.
